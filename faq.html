FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Last updated: Aug 8, 2018

What is Techlab's Teaching Philosophy?

Our core teaching philosophy focuses on nurturing learning in kids through self motivation and hands-on project work in small-size classroom experiences. We apply decades of research in the science of learning and a well defined curriculum to achieve outcomes working with students.


Who are your instructors and teaching assitants?

Our instructors are working professionals at high-tech companies(incl. Google, Intel, Oracle) and college students with Computer and engineering majors. Our staff is trained and we emphasize paying attention to each student's individualized learning needs. We also include teaching assistants in a few classes to assist instructors.


What if my student attended your summer classes?

Our focus is to ensure continued learning through practice to grasp the fundamentals and build on skills. We provide grade-level recommendations. Still need guidance? For a summer-camp student, we offer a complementary evaluation session, to match learning tracks with your student's knowledge and needs. Please email us at info@techlabeducation.com


What discount do I get for enrolling for the entire Fall session?

We offer a 20% discount if you sign up and pay for the entire Fall session. Discounted Full Fall payment is $640 (Tynkerer, Intermediate tracks) and $720 (Advanced track).


Need to customize learning for my student or group of students?

We believe in providing flexible learning paths for each student. We offer one-on-one and small group private tutoring. Including opportunity to engage in an Independent Study Project at Techlab
