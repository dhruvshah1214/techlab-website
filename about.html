we make makers

Our mission is to make technology education accessible & fun for all kids. We provide hands-on training for boys and girls who want to learn, tinker and design.

We are passionate about nurturing the next generation of entrepreneurs, makers, and problem solvers. Our tutors and founders work closely with students to make their dreams come true.
